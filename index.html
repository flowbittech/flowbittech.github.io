FAQ

Who is this course for?

This course is for those who want to learn android app development to transition into a new career or to build an additional skill.

How do I apply for the course?

Please submit the form with your details and we will get in touch with you to take this further.

How much will the course cost overall?

The course will cost Rs. 15,000 which needs to be paid in installments. You can appear for a quiz as well to get scholorship for the course.

Can I do this course at my own pace?

The program has a structured schedule and requires your active participation.

How can I get scholorship for this course?

You can appear for a quiz and if your marks cross a particular cut-off then you will be eligible for 30%-100% scholorship.

What if I miss a live session?

You are encouraged to attend the sessions LIVE to participate in discussions. However, you will have access to all class recordings.

When & where are the classes conducted?

We will conduct live classes on our own platform. Also will conduct activities like Q&A, mentor conversations etc which are optional for you participate in.

Do I need to know programming?

Yes, you need to have some basic knowledge of Java programming language.
